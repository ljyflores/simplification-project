Jerilderie  is a small, rural town in the southern Riverina region of New South Wales, Australia. It is part of the Murrumbidgee Local Government Area. At the [1]2021 census, Jerilderie had a population of 922 people.It can be found along the Newell Highway 674 kilometres (419 mi) south-west of Sydney and 45 kilometres (28 mi) north of the Victorian state border. Jerilderie's postcode is 2716. This is also where Ned Kelly and his Gang robbed the 'Jerilderie Bank'.
