Darcey & Stacey is an American reality television show that premiered on TLC on August 16, 2020. The series documents their family life, professional endeavors, love and relationships in Middletown, Connecticut. The show airs on TLC on Sundays at 10:00 pm. Darcey & Stacey: Inside the Episode showed behind-the-scenes footage about each episode and it aired on TLC on Mondays.
On October 27, 2020, TV Fanatic reported that the series was renewed for a second season after a "record-breaking freshman run". The second season premiered on July 19, 2021, and its after-show Darcey & Stacey: Pillow Talk premiered on July 23, 2021. The third season premiered on January 10, 2022. The fourth season of Darcey & Stacey premiered on January 23, 2023.
