The name Lane has been used for eight tropical cyclones in the Eastern Pacific Ocean.

Tropical Storm Lane (1978)
Tropical Storm Lane (1982)
Hurricane Lane (1988)
Hurricane Lane (1994)
Hurricane Lane (2000)
Hurricane Lane (2006) – a strong Category 3 hurricane that made landfall in the Mexican state of Sinaloa
Hurricane Lane (2012)
Hurricane Lane (2018) – a powerful Category 5 hurricane that weakened as it approached Hawaii
