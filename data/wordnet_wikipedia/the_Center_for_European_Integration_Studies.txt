The Center for European Integration Studies (German: Zentrum für Europäische Integrationsforschung, ZEI) is a transdisciplinary research and post-graduate education institute at the University of Bonn. ZEI has participated in research, policy advice, and dialogue between science and practice since its establishment in 1995. ZEI directors are Prof. Dr. Ludger Kühnhardt and Prof. Dr. Christian Koenig.
