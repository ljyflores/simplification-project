The Hinterrhein (German: [ˈhɪntɐˌʁaɪn] (listen); Italian: Reno Posteriore; Sutsilvan: Ragn Posteriur; Sursilvan: Rein Posteriur; Rumantsch Grischun, Vallader, and Puter: Rain Posteriur; Surmiran: Ragn Posteriour; "Posterior Rhine") is one of the two initial tributaries of the Rhine (shorter in length but bigger by volume) rising in the canton of Graubünden in Switzerland.
