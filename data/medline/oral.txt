It's important to take care of your mouth and teeth starting in childhood. If you don't, you could have problems with your teeth and gums - like cavities or even tooth loss. Here's how to keep your mouth and teeth healthy:Brush your teeth twice a day with a fluoride toothpasteClean between your teeth every day with floss or another type of between-the-teeth cleanerSnack smart - limit sugary snacksDon't smoke or chew tobaccoSee your dentist or oral health professional regularlyNIH: National Institute of Dental and Craniofacial Research
